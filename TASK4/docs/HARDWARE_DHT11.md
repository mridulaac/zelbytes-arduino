## Arduino Uno

Working in the Project:

* Receives temperature and humidity data from the DHT11 sensor.
* Processes the sensor readings.
* Controls the timing of data acquisition.
* Displays the readings on the Serial Monitor.

  
Features:

* ATmega328P microcontroller.
* 14 digital I/O pins and 6 analog input pins.
* Operates at 5 V with a 16 MHz clock speed.
* Supports USB programming and serial communication.

## DHT11 Sensor

Working in the Project:

* Measures ambient temperature.
* Measures relative humidity.
* Sends the measured data digitally to the Arduino Uno.
* Updates sensor readings once every two seconds.

Features:

* Temperature measurement range: 0–50°C.
* Humidity measurement range: 20–90% RH.
* Operates at 3.3–5.5 V.
* Digital output with simple single-wire communication.
